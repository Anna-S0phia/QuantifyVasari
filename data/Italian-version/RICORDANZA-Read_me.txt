RICORDANZA

Vasaris notes for memorising specific artworks, places and events. 

Every entry starts with „Ricordo come…“ = „I remember that…“ and a date, e.g. „11 dicembre 1532“. 

The entries are chronologically ordered. 

The years are split by [line break - line break - year - line break - line break]

The page numbers of the original source are given in the format [line break - X - line break - line break]
X = „c.“ + page number (+ „v“ if verso / back)
